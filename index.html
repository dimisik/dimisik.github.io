About Me

My name is Dimitris Sikeridis and I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of New Mexico, since August 2016. My advisor is professor Michael Devetsikiotis and I am a member of the IoT Lab. I obtained my Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Patras in July 2016. My research interests lie in the areas of Internet of Things (IoT), M2M/D2D Wireless Communications, Distributed Systems, and Machine Learning for Smart Infrastructures and Cyber-Physical systems.
